Full-width slider at the bottom of the controls panel. Controls how much of the body chain joins the head tilt.

Flexibility + body chain activation
50–70% — classic Zouk head roll. 90–100% — full body wave with hips.
10
Viewport — Left EdgeSpeed Slider
Mobile & Desktop

Tap ⚡ SPEED in the upper-left corner of the canvas to show/hide the vertical speed slider.

Speed Slider

The slider scales the entire simulation speed from 0% (frozen) to 150% (fast). Everything slows uniformly — body, head, hair, legs — like true slow motion.

Snap zone: Dragging within ±6% of 100% automatically snaps to exactly 100% and the tick mark lights up gold.
